Traditional Cuisine of Congo Brazzaville

Discover the delicious flavors of Congolese traditional dishes.

Saka-Saka

A popular dish made from cassava leaves, often cooked with peanuts and served with rice or fufu.

Pondu

A flavorful dish made with cassava leaves, palm oil, and spices, commonly eaten with rice or plantains.

Maboke

Fresh fish marinated in local spices, wrapped in banana leaves, and slow-cooked to perfection.

Kwanga

A traditional staple made from fermented cassava, often served as a side dish.
