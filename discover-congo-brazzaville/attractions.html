Top Attractions in Congo Brazzaville

Explore the breathtaking landscapes, historical sites, and cultural gems of the country.

Lesio-Louna Wildlife Reserve

A sanctuary for gorillas and other wildlife, offering visitors a chance to explore nature up close.

Basilique Sainte-Anne

One of the most iconic religious landmarks in Brazzaville, known for its unique architecture.

Loukoléla River

A stunning river that offers breathtaking views and boat excursions.

Diosso Gorge

Also known as the “Grand Canyon of Congo,” this stunning landscape is perfect for adventure lovers.
